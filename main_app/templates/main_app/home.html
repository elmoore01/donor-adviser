What’s your Passion?

Most people think of large foundations when they hear philanthropy, but individual donors are the lifeblood of the nonprofit sector. Donors share a passion for the mission of the organizations they support and build strong relationships with their leaders. Thus, almost two-thirds of nonprofit leaders say that they would prefer to receive more of their funding from individual donors.

Educate California

Educate California’s mission is to help families prepare their children for college, careers and life after high school, by offering in-depth workshops and webinars for parents.

California Police Activities League

The mission of California PAL is uniting law enforcement and communities by providing educational, athletic and life enriching youth programs that will develop discipline, positive self-image, mutual trust and respect.

Executive Service Corps of Southern California

At Executive Service Corps of Southern California, we empower nonprofits, helping them achieve their missions through high quality, affordable coaching and consulting delivered by executive-level volunteers.
