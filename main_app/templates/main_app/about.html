What Is Donor Adviser?


There are more than 1.7 million registered nonprofits in the United States with one common goal - raise enough money to offer services to benefit their communities. Donor Adviserâ„¢ safely connects individual donors with nonprofits whose missions reflect their philanthropic goals


Most people think of large foundations when they hear philanthropy, but individual donors are the lifeblood of the nonprofit sector! Donor Adviser is a national database of nonprofit organizations that are seeking support from individuals, corporations, foundations, donor advised funds and governmental agencies.


Sites like Guidestar, Charity Navigator and Candid provide ratings for charities, but only Donor Adviser helps match donors with nonprofits and allows supporters to donate to and leave reviews for their favorite organizations.
