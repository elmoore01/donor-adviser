Why Donor Adviser?

Because we don't just list your organization, we promote your organization to a much larger community of donors than one nonprofit can reach.


Rather than competing for limited funds from a small number of foundations and corporations, our algorithims enable nonprofits to take advantage of governing dynamics. Organizations listed on Donor Adviser agree to utilize their social media channels to help create a community of giving that will benefit not only their nonprofit, but the entire nonprofit sector!


By combining an online platform that showcases and promotes the good works of nonprofits, with a collaborative effort to increase the number of qualified donors, nonprofits will be able to reach more potential donors than by conducting outreach on their own.


Further, by enabling and encouraging donors to cover the costs associated with processing credit card donations, we ensure nonprofits receive 100% of the total amount donated by supporters.
